Ok, so what is Project Play anyway?

Project Play is a celebration of gaming and giving back. Gamers of all ages from all over the region come together to create new friendships and rekindle old rivalries (all in good fun, of course).

Since day zero, Project Play has been strongly focused on our community. 100% of the money from ticket sales have been used to purchase video and board games for community centers and family crisis centers right here in London, Ontario.
