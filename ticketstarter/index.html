Here's how it works! You buy a ticket and your name gets entered in a draw for loot. As certain numbers of tickets are purchased, a loot tier is unlocked. All people who have already bought tickets get a chance to win. If you do win, you collect your prize and your name is removed from the draw. The more pre-event tickets we sell, the more tiers we unlock and the more prizes you can win!
